Technology Overview

Why is Generation Suppression Technology Needed?

With advances in deep learning, it has become possible to precisely replicate
anyone's voice from just a short audio sample.

01
🎭
Voice Cloning Threat
Modern AI voice synthesis can realistically reproduce a person's voice from just a short audio sample.
Such technology is increasingly being exploited for voice phishing and unauthorized content creation.
02
⚡
Cloning Attack Method
Attackers input publicly available voice data into TTS models to easily generate fake voices that sound like the original speaker. This poses a very high risk as anyone can attempt it without special equipment.
03
🛡️
Our Technology's Solution
We embed imperceptible protection signals into audio. When TTS is trained on protected audio, the synthesizer fails to reproduce the speaker's voice.
